OJT Experience

On March 13, 2023, I officially started my on-the-job training at RWeb Solutions, marking the beginning of an incredible learning journey. On the first day, I found myself feeling overwhelmed as I entered the professional world. Initially, I was taken aback by the task assigned to me as it seemed unrelated to my course of study. I was given a marketing assignment with a focus on finding potential clients. However, I decided to take proactive steps and approached my supervisor with a request to align my tasks more closely with my coursework. To my delight, my supervisor entrusted me with additional responsibilities that allowed me to delve into my passion for designing.
As the training progressed, I embraced the opportunity to modify a website template, design a website, and work on its front-end development. The initial task of sourcing potential clients for three days provided me with a deeper understanding of the significance of identifying target audiences, despite the challenges I faced when unsure about the specific demographics.

The assignments involving IT, though, are where I really develop. Working on website design, where I was at ease and sure of my skills, gave me a great deal of satisfaction. However, I also ran into difficulties while attempting to understand complicated front-end elements of design, which forced me to broaden my knowledge and problem-solving abilities.
Throughout the duration of my on-the-job training at RWeb Solutions, I not only gained invaluable knowledge and technical expertise but also experienced significant personal and professional growth. The exposure to real-world projects and working within a professional team taught me the importance of adaptability, effective communication, and collaboration. I learned to embrace challenges as opportunities for growth and to push my boundaries to deliver high-quality results.
Overall, being a part of RWeb Solutions proved to be an enriching experience that deepened my understanding of the industry and refined my skills. It provided me with a solid foundation to build upon as I progress in my career. I am grateful for the opportunity to have been a part of such a dynamic and supportive team, and I am excited to leverage the knowledge and experiences gained during my training as I embark on new professional endeavors.
